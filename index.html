I am Geeta Palle, a dedicated professional with a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Ahmedabad Institute of Technology (GTU, 2014). I have developed strong expertise in data management, analysis, and visualization, with exposure to both the corporate and government sectors.

I recently completed a Data Analyst Internship at AI Variant (Oct 2024 – Jul 2025), where I worked with Excel, Power BI, Tableau, MySQL, Python, and R to transform raw data into actionable insights, build interactive dashboards, and support data-driven decision-making.

In addition, I bring prior experience in data processing, reporting, and customer support, along with a technical foundation in embedded systems, networking, and PCB design.

With strong skills in Excel, Power BI, Tableau, MySQL, Python, and R, combined with my experience in data management, reporting, analysis, customer service support, and technical problem-solving, I am eager to contribute my analytical and problem-solving abilities to help organizations make better, data-driven decisions.

I believe everyday is a new opportunity to learn something new. I am currently trying to put all my knowledge into action by doing projects in the analytics field.

My aim in life is to be better person than I was yesterday. I would like to use my knowledge and skills to give back to the society in any way possible. I also believe that good things take time.

Work Experience

I worked as a Data Analyst Intern at AI Variant (Oct 2024 – Jul 2025), where I gained practical experience in Excel, Power BI, Tableau, MySQL, Python, and R. My role involved analyzing datasets, identifying patterns, and building interactive dashboards and reports to provide meaningful business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

Previously, I worked part-time as a Data Entry Specialist at the Office of the Insurance Ombudsman, Ahmedabad (Jan 2024 – Mar 2024), where I was responsible for managing and entering large volumes of insurance claims and customer data with high accuracy. I conducted regular data audits, reducing errors by 15% through validation processes, maintained both physical and electronic records in compliance with regulations, and generated reports that assisted management in decision-making.

From Mar 2021 to Dec 2023, I was engaged part-time as a Support Associate to the Development Officer at LIC of India in the Back Office Department. In this role, I handled data processing, analysis, and documentation, managed comprehensive records, and assisted in resolving customer inquiries by retrieving and providing relevant data promptly.

Earlier in my career, I worked as a Production Technician at Mantra Softech Pvt. Ltd. (Jul 2017 – Jul 2018), where I gained technical exposure to PCB testing, schematic designing, and soldering in the SMT department. I collaborated with cross-functional teams to optimize production processes and ensure the delivery of reliable products.
